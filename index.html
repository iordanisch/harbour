Welcome to the Harbour Cottage


Nestled on a peaceful bay overlooking Loch Carron, Harbour Cottage offers an idyllic setting for your visit to the Highlands. Yards from the beach in a quiet setting with glorious views all round. Perfect base for touring or just relaxing.


Sleeping from 2 to 6, Harbour Cottage has been recently re-roofed, double glazed, smoke detector system installed and refurbished, the cottage is idyllic for a romantic get-away or with its enclosed gardens and beach location is a perfect family base.
