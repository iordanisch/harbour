Welcome to the Harbour Cottage


Sleeps from 2 to 6 people

A much loved and popular base for our many returning guests...


Nestled in a peaceful bay overlooking Loch Carron, Harbour Cottage offers an idyllic setting for your visit to the Highlands. Situated yards from the beach in a quiet setting with glorious views all around Lochcarron. Harbour Cottage is a perfect base for touring or just relaxing. And what a view to relax with...


Facilities



Accommodation

The cottage can cater for 2 guests downstairs in a king size loch-facing en-suite bedroom or for up to 4 more guests upstairs in a twin (side view) and loch facing double room with wc/bathroom.

Dog friendly

We love dogs and its a perfect holiday for our 4 legged friends, but we can only accept one dog at a time and request that it is well-behaved, doesn't chew and is house-trained.

Free WiFi

There is free wi fi access and Freeview TV in the sitting area and kitchen.

Private garden

The area around the cottage is enclosed with seating areas at the front to enjoy the view down the loch and at the rear overlooking the garden and hills.

Private parking

There is off-road private parking in front of the cottage or inside the gates if preferred.


The cottage is a perfect base for a romantic escape or a family holiday with its enclosed garden and lochside peaceful location - offering amazing views down the loch and of the mountains around.

Lots to see and do


Visit Applecross

Drive over the world-famous Bealach-na-ba to the Applecross Inn (you must book); the nearby Walled Garden, then to the Sands Beach (near the Ministry of Defence submarine testing station). ‘The Bealach’ is particularly famous for being a brutal climb amongst cyclists. Dare you try it?


Learn more


Eilean Donan

Eilean Donan is a small tidal island situated at the confluence of three sea lochs in the western Highlands of Scotland, about 1 kilometre from the village of Dornie. Within 30 minutes drive from Lochcarron can you experience one of Scotland's most iconic castles.


Learn more


Plockton

Plockton is a nearby fishing village with amazing seafood, a brilliant seal-watching tour, and a little island you can only walk to in low-tide. There is a lovely animal farm, and if you go along from Harbor Cottage (left once you hit the ‘main’ road), you’ll end up at Strome Castle, where you can just about see Plockton with binoculars.


Learn more


Attadale Gardens

These lush, well-kept gardens have phenomenal flora, fantastic fauna…and cheap coffee. If you turn right (rather than left into the gardens), and follow the road before going up the obvious path, you can recreate the annual Highland Games race!
